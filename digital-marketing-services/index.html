Frequently Asked Questions About Digital Marketing Services for Small Businesses

What smart business owners want to know before investing in professional marketing.

What digital marketing services does Salois Digital offer?

Salois Digital provides four core digital marketing services designed to work together as one system: AI Voice Agents that answer every call and book appointments 24/7, Google Ads management that targets high-intent searches and tracks real conversions, organic SEO and AIO optimization to build long-term visibility in both traditional search and AI-generated answers, and conversion-optimized website development. Unlike agencies that sell one-off tactics in isolation, we connect them, so no lead slips through and every campaign ties back to booked appointments — not just vanity clicks.

How much do digital marketing services cost for a small business?

Our packages are built for small business budgets, not the kind of agency-level fees that make small owners feel like they've been sold something they'll never use. You'll get a clear scope of work with transparent pricing and a detailed explanation of what's included — no surprise charges, no 12-month lock-in contracts. Pricing varies by service mix, but most clients start with a combination of Google Ads plus our AI Voice Agent to start capturing leads immediately. Visit our pricing page for details and to see exactly what your investment will look like.

Do I need all four services, or can I start with just one?

You can absolutely start with the services that address your most urgent need. If you're running ads but missing calls, the AI Voice Agent alone can recover massive lost revenue. If you're not showing up online at all, SEO and Google Ads paired together get you visible fast. That said, the full system delivers the strongest results because each component reinforces the others — ads drive traffic, the landing page converts, the AI answers, and the appointment gets booked. We'll recommend the right starting point based on your situation.

What makes Salois Digital different from other digital marketing agencies?

Most agencies are a patchwork of disconnected services — they'll build you a website here, run some ads there, maybe post on social media once a week. But none of it connects. Salois Digital is built around one principle: every marketing dollar should lead to a booked appointment. Our proprietary AI Voice Agent ensures no lead falls through the cracks. Our tracking shows you which ads produce real revenue (not vanity metrics). And our reporting is transparent — you see every call, every conversation, every lead. It's a marketing system, not a collection of random tactics.

How long does it take to see results from digital marketing?

It depends on the channel. Google Ads produce results immediately — you can see booked leads within the first two weeks of launch. SEO is a longer play, typically 60 to 90 days before you see meaningful ranking improvements, but the traffic it generates is free and compounds over time. Our AI Voice Agent starts producing value the moment it's live — every missed call it catches is a lead that would have otherwise gone to your competitor. Most clients are fully onboarded in 14 days or less.

Absolutely — we encourage it. Demo our AI Voice Agent right now to experience it firsthand. It picks up instantly, greets you naturally, and walks through the same conversation your customers would experience. Most business owners are surprised by how natural and human-like it sounds.

No. Salois Digital does not lock clients into long-term agreements. We work on a month-to-month basis because we believe our results speak for themselves.
