Frequently Asked Questions About Digital Marketing for Medical Practices

Answers medical practice owners ask most about getting more patients, managing ads, and growing with AI.

How do medical practices get more patients online?

The most effective approach combines three things: showing up when patients search for care (Google Ads + local SEO), answering every call that comes in (AI Voice Agent), and making it easy to book an appointment (conversion-optimized website with online scheduling). Most medical practices lose patients not because they lack visibility, but because calls go to voicemail during lunch breaks, after hours, and when the front desk is busy with patients already in the office. Fixing that single leak — the missed call — is the fastest way to grow your patient base without increasing your ad budget.

What is the best type of digital marketing for dentists, chiropractors, and medical specialists?

Google Ads targeting high-intent searches like "dentist accepting new patients near me," "chiropractor back pain [city]," or "dermatologist appointment" are the fastest path to new patients because you're reaching people who actively need care right now. Combine that with local SEO to appear in the map pack, an optimized Google Business Profile loaded with reviews and practice photos, and an AI Voice Agent that answers every call — and you've built a system that captures patients from every angle while your competitors lose them to voicemail.

How much do Google Ads cost for medical practices?

Cost per click for medical keywords varies significantly by specialty and location — dental keywords typically run $5 to $30 per click, chiropractic $8 to $25, and specialty medical terms can range from $10 to $50. What matters more than cost per click is cost per new patient, and the lifetime value of that patient to your practice. A $20 click that turns into a patient worth $3,000 to $10,000 over their lifetime is an extraordinary return. Salois Digital tracks every call and booked appointment back to the ad that generated it so you always know your true cost per acquisition.

Why do medical practices miss so many patient calls?

Front desk staff are simultaneously checking in patients, verifying insurance, answering questions, and processing paperwork. When the phone rings during peak hours — or after 5pm when the office closes — calls go to voicemail. Industry data shows that medical practices miss 30 to 50 percent of inbound calls during business hours, and nearly 100 percent after hours. Since patients who can't reach you simply call the next practice on Google, every missed call is a lost patient. An AI Voice Agent answers within two seconds, 24/7, eliminating this problem entirely.

Is SEO worth it for local medical practices?

Absolutely. Healthcare is inherently local — patients search for providers in their area and rarely travel far for routine care. Local SEO puts your practice in the Google map pack, which is often the first thing searchers see. Unlike paid ads, organic rankings generate free, high-quality patient leads month after month. For medical practices with high patient lifetime values, even a few extra organic leads per month can represent significant revenue. We optimize your website content, Google Business Profile, online directory listings, and review strategy to build lasting visibility.

How does the AI Voice Agent work for medical practices?

When your front desk can't answer — during peak hours, lunch breaks, after 5pm, or on weekends — the AI picks up within two seconds. It greets the caller professionally, captures their name, contact information, reason for calling, insurance details, and preferred appointment times. It can answer common questions about your services, accepted insurances, and office hours, then book the appointment directly into your scheduling system. Every call is recorded and transcribed so your staff can review patient details before confirming. It works 24/7, including evenings and weekends when patients are most likely to search for new providers.

What's the difference between Salois Digital and other marketing agencies for medical practices?

Most healthcare marketing agencies build a website, run some ads, and send you a report full of impressions and click numbers. The problem is none of that matters if half your calls go to voicemail. Salois Digital builds a connected patient acquisition system: ads drive high-intent patients to your practice, the AI Voice Agent answers and books every call, your landing pages make it easy to schedule, and SEO compounds your visibility over time. We track new patient appointments, not vanity metrics — because that's what actually grows your practice.

How fast can a medical practice start getting new patients from digital marketing?

With Google Ads, patient calls can start within the first week of launch. Most practices see their first new patient appointments booked within two weeks of going live. SEO results build more gradually — typically 60 to 90 days for meaningful ranking improvements — but the combination of paid ads and AI call handling means you're booking new patients from day one while organic visibility builds in the background. Our 14-day onboarding process gets your entire system live as fast as possible.
