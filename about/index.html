The Person Behind the Platform

Built to Solve a Problem Small Businesses Shouldn’t Lose

Salois Digital exists because too many good businesses lose customers to missed calls, wasted ad spend, and marketing that doesn’t connect. We built the system that fixes all three.

Michael Salois Founder & CEO

The Problem I Kept Seeing

I started working in digital marketing because I saw the same thing happening over and over: small business owners spending money on ads, building websites, posting on social media — and still not getting the results they were paying for.

The problem wasn’t the ads. It wasn’t the website. It was what happened after someone clicked. They called and got voicemail. They filled out a form and nobody followed up. They were ready to book — and the business wasn’t ready to catch them.

I realized that marketing without a system to capture every lead is just burning money. So I built that system.

What Salois Digital Actually Is

Salois Digital is a connected growth platform for local businesses. At the core is an AI Voice Agent that answers every call, 24/7 — qualifying leads, booking appointments, and capturing the customers your team physically can’t get to. Around that, we layer Google Ads that drive high-intent traffic, SEO that builds long-term visibility, and websites engineered to convert.

Every piece feeds the next. Your ads bring in calls. The AI answers those calls. Your dashboard shows what’s working. And you see more booked jobs — not more vanity metrics.

How We Operate

These aren’t slogans on a wall. They’re the commitments that shape every client relationship.

Full Transparency

You see everything: every call, every ad dollar, every lead. No black-box reporting, no vanity metrics. We track the numbers that actually matter — booked appointments and cost per lead — and put them in front of you in plain English.

No Contracts, Ever

We don’t lock you in because we don’t need to. If our system is booking you more jobs and growing your revenue, you’ll want to keep working with us. If it’s not, you should be free to leave. We earn your business every month.

Speed Matters

Most clients are onboarded within 14 days. When you reach out, you hear back the same day — from Michael, not a chatbot. When there’s a problem, we fix it. When there’s an opportunity, we move on it. Speed is a competitive advantage and we treat it like one.

Results Over Activity

We don’t measure success by how many social posts went out or how many impressions an ad got. We measure it by how many appointments got booked, how much revenue came in, and whether your phone is ringing with the right customers. If it’s not moving the needle, we change it.

Most agencies give you pieces. We give you a machine.

One Connected System

Your AI Voice Agent, your ads, your SEO, and your website all talk to each other. When an ad drives a call and the AI books an appointment, you see the entire chain in one dashboard. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Other agencies give you a phone number and hope someone answers. We give you an AI Voice Agent that picks up every call, every time — at midnight, on Sunday, during the holiday rush. 80% of callers who hit voicemail call a competitor instead. We eliminate that problem entirely.
