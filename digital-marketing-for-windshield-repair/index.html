Frequently Asked Questions About Windshield Repair Marketing

What auto glass business owners ask most about getting more repair jobs, growing revenue, and using AI.

How do windshield repair businesses get more customers from Google?

Google Ads capture drivers at the exact moment they need glass repair — searches like “windshield repair near me,” “auto glass replacement [city],” and “chip repair same day” are extremely high-intent. Local SEO ensures you show up in the map pack. The critical piece is answering those calls — drivers with fresh chips want same-day service, and if they reach voicemail, they call the next company instantly.

Why do auto glass businesses lose so many jobs to missed calls?

Most windshield repair businesses operate with small teams doing mobile work. When you’re on a job site replacing a windshield, you can’t answer the phone for 60 to 90 minutes. For shop-based operations, the tech doing the install is often the same person who answers phones. Our AI Voice Agent eliminates this completely — every call answered instantly, every job captured.

What auto glass services convert best with Google Ads?

Emergency and insurance-related searches convert best. “Windshield repair near me,” “auto glass replacement same day,” “windshield chip repair,” and “free windshield replacement insurance” produce strong conversion rates. We target both insurance-claim customers and out-of-pocket payers with different messaging and landing pages.

How does the AI handle windshield repair calls?

The AI captures the damage type (chip, crack, full break), damage size and location, vehicle year/make/model, whether the caller has insurance, and their preferred repair time. For mobile service, it captures the caller’s location. It can explain the difference between chip repair and full replacement and provide general pricing information.

Can the AI handle insurance-related questions?

Yes. The AI is configured to ask if the caller has comprehensive coverage, capture their insurance provider, and explain that many policies cover windshield repair with zero deductible. It routes insurance claims and out-of-pocket jobs differently so your team can process them efficiently.

How do I compete with national auto glass chains?

National chains have brand recognition but struggle with responsiveness and customer service. Independent auto glass businesses win on speed, convenience (especially mobile service), and personal attention. Google Ads and local SEO put you in front of local drivers who want fast, reliable service. Our AI ensures you answer faster than any chain’s call center.

How much should an auto glass business spend on Google Ads?

Windshield repair CPCs typically range from $8 to $25. With chip repairs at $50 to $100 and full replacements at $200 to $500+, even a modest budget generates strong returns. We recommend starting with a budget that generates 20 to 40 new calls per month, then scaling based on your conversion rate and average job value.

What makes Salois Digital different for auto glass businesses?

We build the complete system: Google Ads for urgent glass repair searches, local SEO for map pack visibility, and an AI Voice Agent that answers every call — even when you’re on a mobile job. We track repair jobs booked and revenue, not clicks.
