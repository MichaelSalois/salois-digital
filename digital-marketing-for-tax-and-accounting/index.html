Frequently Asked Questions About Digital Marketing for Tax & Accounting Firms

Answers tax & accounting firms ask most about getting more clients and growing with AI.

How do accounting firms get more clients online?

The most effective approach combines Google Ads targeting people searching for CPAs and accountants, local SEO for map pack visibility, a professional website showcasing your credentials and specialties, and an AI Voice Agent that answers every call — especially during tax season when your phones are busiest. Most accounting firms lose clients because calls go unanswered during February through April. An AI that answers instantly captures those high-value recurring clients.

What is the best way to market a tax and accounting firm?

Google Ads targeting "CPA near me," "tax preparation services," and "accountant for small business" reach prospects ready to hire. Combine with a credentials-focused landing page, Google Business Profile optimization, local SEO for year-round visibility, and an AI Voice Agent that handles tax season call surges without missing a single prospect.

How much do Google Ads cost for accounting firms?

Cost per click for accounting keywords typically ranges from $5 to $25. With average client values of $3,000 to $8,000+ per year in recurring fees, even a few new clients per month delivers exceptional ROI. Salois Digital tracks cost per booked consultation and new client acquisition, not just clicks.

Why do accounting firms miss so many client calls during tax season?

January through April, call volume spikes 300 to 500 percent while your team is heads-down preparing returns. Every phone line rings simultaneously. After-hours calls from business owners who work during the day go to voicemail. The AI Voice Agent answers every call within two seconds, year-round, handling the surge so your team stays productive.

How does the AI Voice Agent work for accounting firms?

When a prospect calls and your team is unavailable, the AI answers within two seconds. It asks about their service needs — tax preparation, bookkeeping, payroll, advisory — captures their business type, approximate revenue, and urgency. It books a consultation directly into your calendar and can answer questions about your services and process.

Is SEO worth it for accounting firms?

Yes. Business owners search for accountants locally and value trust, credentials, and proximity. Ranking for "best CPA in [city]" generates free, qualified leads month after month. The key is year-round SEO so you're already ranked when tax season demand surges. We optimize your website, Google Business Profile, and directory listings.

How fast can an accounting firm start getting new clients from marketing?

Google Ads generate consultation calls within the first week. Most firms see booked consultations within two weeks of launch. SEO builds over 60 to 90 days. The combination means you're signing new clients immediately while organic visibility compounds.
