Frequently Asked Questions About Digital Marketing for Home Services

Answers contractors and home service business owners ask most about getting more leads and growing revenue.

How do home service businesses get more customers online?

The most effective approach combines three things: showing up when homeowners search (Google Ads + local SEO), answering every call that comes in (AI Voice Agent), and making it easy to book (conversion-optimized website and landing pages). Most contractors lose leads not because they lack traffic, but because calls go to voicemail or their Google Business Profile doesn't rank in the map pack. Fixing those two leaks alone can double or triple the number of booked jobs from your existing marketing budget.

What is the best type of digital marketing for plumbers, HVAC companies, and electricians?

Google Ads is the fastest way to generate leads because it targets people who are actively searching for your service — "emergency plumber near me," "AC repair today," "electrician available now." These are high-intent searches from homeowners who need help immediately and will call the first business they see. Combine that with local SEO (so you show up in the map pack organically) and an AI Voice Agent to answer every call, and you've built a system that converts searchers into paying customers around the clock.

How much do Google Ads cost for home service businesses?

Cost per click for home service keywords typically ranges from $8 to $50 depending on your trade, location, and competition level. Emergency plumbing and HVAC keywords tend to be on the higher end because the job values are higher. What matters more than cost per click is cost per booked job — and that's where most agencies fall short. They'll report clicks and impressions, but they can't tell you which ad generated the $3,000 water heater install. Salois Digital tracks every call and appointment back to the specific ad and keyword that produced it.

Why do contractors miss so many phone leads?

Because the nature of the work puts you on a job site, underground, on a roof, or elbow-deep in a system when the phone rings. Your office staff — if you have any — is often handling existing customers, scheduling, or on another line. Industry data shows that home service businesses miss 40 to 60 percent of inbound calls during peak hours. Each missed call is a homeowner who immediately calls your competitor. An AI Voice Agent solves this by answering instantly, capturing the lead information, qualifying the job, and booking the appointment — all while your team stays focused on the work.

Is SEO worth it for local home service companies?

Absolutely — but it's a long-term play, not a quick fix. Local SEO puts your business in the Google map pack when homeowners search for services in your area. That placement generates free, high-quality leads month after month without paying per click. The key factors are an optimized Google Business Profile, consistent NAP (name, address, phone) across directories, reviews, and a website with location-specific content. We recommend running Google Ads for immediate leads while SEO builds in the background — within 60 to 90 days, you'll have both paid and organic leads flowing in.

How does the AI Voice Agent work for contractors?

When a homeowner calls and your team can't answer, the AI Voice Agent picks up within two seconds. It greets the caller naturally, asks what service they need, captures their name, phone number, address, and job details, determines urgency (routine vs. emergency), and either books an appointment into your calendar or sends a notification to dispatch. The caller never knows they're speaking with AI — and you never lose another lead to voicemail. It works 24/7 including evenings, weekends, and holidays, which is when many emergency calls come in.

What's the difference between Salois Digital and other marketing agencies for contractors?

Most agencies sell you a website, run some ads, and send you a monthly report full of impressions and clicks. Salois Digital builds a connected revenue system: ads drive calls, the AI Voice Agent answers every one of those calls and books the job, and SEO compounds your visibility over time. We track booked appointments — not vanity metrics. And unlike large agencies that manage hundreds of accounts with junior staff, we focus specifically on local service businesses and build campaigns around the keywords and call flows that actually produce revenue in your trade.

How fast can a home service business start getting leads?

With Google Ads, you can see calls within the first week of campaign launch. Our 14-day onboarding process includes a full business audit, campaign build, AI Voice Agent setup, and landing page deployment. By day 14, everything is live and generating leads. SEO results take longer — typically 60 to 90 days for meaningful ranking improvements — but the ads and AI call handling ensure you're capturing revenue from day one while organic visibility builds.
