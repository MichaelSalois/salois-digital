Frequently Asked Questions About Digital Marketing for Local Businesses

Common questions from business owners evaluating marketing partners.

Why should I hire a specialized marketing agency instead of a general one?

A general marketing agency applies the same strategies to every business — the same keyword research, the same ad templates, the same landing page layouts. A specialized agency like Salois Digital already knows what works for your industry. We know which keywords convert for plumbers vs. dentists, which call scripts resonate with auto repair customers vs. med spa clients, and which seasonal patterns drive demand in each vertical. That means faster results, less wasted spend, and campaigns built on proven data instead of guesswork.

What does "appointment-based business" mean and why does it matter for marketing?

An appointment-based business is any business where revenue comes from scheduled interactions — a dental cleaning, a plumbing repair visit, a wedding photography consultation, a tax preparation meeting. The marketing funnel for these businesses is unique: the conversion event is a phone call or online booking, not an e-commerce checkout. This means every part of the marketing system — ads, SEO, website, call handling — needs to be optimized for getting that call and converting it into a confirmed appointment. Generic marketing strategies designed for e-commerce or brand awareness don't work.

How do you customize your approach for each industry?

Customization happens at every level. For Google Ads, we use industry-specific keyword lists built from real search data — "emergency pipe burst" for plumbers, "Botox near me" for med spas, "mobile oil change" for auto service. For the AI Voice Agent, we train conversation flows on the intake questions, objections, and scheduling workflows specific to your business type. For SEO, we optimize for the local search categories and schema markup that matter for your industry. For websites, we build around the conversion patterns that work — an auto repair page has different trust signals than a wedding photographer's portfolio.

Can you help my business if it's in an industry not listed on your site?

Potentially. Our system is built for any local business that depends on phone calls and booked appointments. While we've developed deep specialization in our six core industries, the underlying technology — AI call handling, Google Ads, SEO, and conversion optimization — works for many other service-based businesses. Contact us to discuss your specific situation. If we're a good fit, we'll tell you. If not, we'll tell you that too.

How much does digital marketing cost for a local business?

Digital marketing costs for local businesses vary based on your market's competitiveness, the services you need, and your growth goals. A small-town landscaper and a multi-location dental practice in a major metro have very different needs and budgets. At Salois Digital, we provide transparent pricing that scales with your business — no hidden fees, no surprise charges, and no long-term contracts. Visit our pricing page for package details or request a free consultation for a customized quote.

How do I know if digital marketing is working for my business?

This is where most agencies fail — they report on vanity metrics like impressions and clicks that don't mean anything for your bottom line. Salois Digital tracks the metrics that matter: calls received, calls answered, appointments booked, cost per booked appointment, and revenue generated per marketing dollar spent. Every client gets a real-time dashboard that connects ad spend directly to business outcomes. If a campaign isn't generating booked appointments at an acceptable cost, we see it immediately and optimize.

What if I'm already working with another marketing agency?

That's common — many of our clients come to us after frustrating experiences with agencies that over-promised and under-delivered. We're happy to audit your current marketing setup for free and show you specifically where leads are being lost. If you're getting results and just want to add our AI Voice Agent to capture missed calls, we can do that too. There's no pressure to switch everything at once — start where the biggest gap is and expand from there.

How quickly can I get started?

Most clients are fully onboarded and live within 14 days. Week one covers your business audit, AI Voice Agent setup, and Google Business Profile optimization. Week two gets your landing pages live, ad campaigns running, and the entire system connected. Because Google Ads generate traffic immediately, most businesses see their first AI-handled calls within the first week of launch. No long setup periods, no months of "strategic planning" before anything happens — we build fast because your leads aren't waiting.
