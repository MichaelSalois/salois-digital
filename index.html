Frequently Asked Questions About Digital Marketing for Small Businesses

Answers to the questions local business owners ask most about getting more customers, managing ads, and growing with AI.

How is Salois Digital different from a typical marketing agency?

Most marketing agencies sell you isolated services — a website here, some social media posts there, maybe a Google Ads campaign that nobody monitors. The problem is none of it connects. Salois Digital builds a fully connected growth system: an AI Voice Agent that answers every call and books appointments, Google Ads that drive high-intent traffic, organic SEO that builds long-term visibility, and a Google Business Profile optimized to show up in the local map pack. Every piece feeds the next, so your ad dollars aren't wasted when a lead calls and nobody picks up. That's the difference between spending money on marketing and actually getting a return on it.

What is an AI Voice Agent and how does it help my business get more customers?

An AI Voice Agent is an intelligent phone system that answers calls on behalf of your business — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. When a potential customer calls and you're busy with a job, on another line, or closed for the day, the AI picks up instantly. It greets the caller naturally, captures their name, service need, and contact information, qualifies the lead, and can book the appointment directly into your calendar. For small businesses that depend on phone calls — like plumbers, HVAC companies, dentists, auto repair shops, and salons — this means you never lose a lead to voicemail again. Studies show that 80% of callers who reach voicemail will hang up and call your competitor instead. The AI Voice Agent eliminates that problem entirely.

Will the AI Voice Agent replace my receptionist or office staff?

No — and that's not the goal. The AI Voice Agent is designed to catch the calls your team misses: after-hours calls, calls that come in while the front desk is busy, and overflow during peak seasons. It handles the repetitive intake work — gathering caller details, answering common questions, and scheduling — so your staff can focus on the customers already in front of them. Think of it as adding a team member who never takes a break, never calls in sick, and works evenings and weekends at a fraction of the cost of a new hire.

How do Google Ads help small businesses get more customers?

Google Ads put your business at the top of search results the moment someone is actively looking for your service. When a homeowner searches "emergency plumber near me" or a patient searches "dentist accepting new patients," your ad appears before any organic result. The key is targeting high-intent keywords — the searches people make when they're ready to book, not just browsing. At Salois Digital, we build campaigns specifically around these high-intent, service-specific searches, optimize bids using AI, and track every call and booked appointment back to the ad that generated it. Combined with our AI Voice Agent answering every call that comes in, you turn ad spend directly into booked revenue instead of missed opportunities.

What is SEO and why does my small business need it?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of making your business visible in Google's unpaid search results — the listings that appear below the ads and inside the local map pack. When someone searches "best HVAC company in [your city]" or "hair salon near me," SEO determines whether your business shows up on page one or gets buried on page five where nobody looks. Local SEO is especially critical for service-based businesses because nearly half of all Google searches have local intent. We optimize your website content, Google Business Profile, online directory listings, and review strategy so that your business ranks where your customers are already searching — without paying per click.

I'm already running ads — why am I not getting enough leads?

This is one of the most common problems we solve. In most cases, the ads themselves are generating clicks and calls — but the leads are leaking out of your system before they convert. The biggest culprits: calls going to voicemail because the front desk is busy, slow response times that let the lead go cold, landing pages that don't make it easy to take action, or no system to follow up with callers who didn't book. Salois Digital fixes the entire pipeline — not just the top of the funnel. Our AI Voice Agent catches every missed call, our landing pages are built to convert, and our reporting shows you exactly which ads are producing booked appointments versus wasted clicks.

What types of businesses does Salois Digital work with?

We specialize in local, appointment-based businesses across six core industries: home services (plumbing, HVAC, electrical, roofing, landscaping, pest control), beauty and wellness (hair salons, barber shops, med spas, massage studios, gyms), automotive (auto repair, car detailing, tire shops, mobile mechanics), event services (wedding planners, venues, photographers, DJs, rental companies), medical (dentistry, orthodontics, chiropractic, dermatology, medical specialties), and financial services (tax and accounting firms, insurance agencies, mortgage brokers, credit repair). If your business depends on the phone ringing and appointments getting booked, our system is built for you.
