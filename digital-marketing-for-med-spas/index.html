Frequently Asked Questions About Med Spa Marketing

What med spa owners ask most about attracting aesthetic clients, handling high-value leads, and growing with AI.

How do med spas get more high-value aesthetic clients online?

The most effective method is capturing people at the moment they're researching specific treatments. When someone searches "Botox near me," "lip filler [city]," or "CoolSculpting cost," they're actively considering treatment and ready to book a consultation. Google Ads put your med spa at the top of these searches immediately. Combined with our AI Voice Agent answering every call and booking consultations, you convert a dramatically higher percentage of searchers into paying patients compared to med spas that rely on voicemail or contact forms.

Why do med spas lose consultation leads to missed calls?

Med spas have a unique staffing challenge — providers are in treatment rooms for 30 to 60 minutes at a time and can't take calls. Front desk staff get overwhelmed during peak hours, especially when multiple calls come in while they're checking patients in. A missed call from someone researching Botox or fillers doesn't just lose one appointment — it loses the lifetime value of that client, which can be $3,000 to $10,000 or more. The AI Voice Agent ensures every high-value lead is captured immediately.

What med spa treatments convert best with Google Ads?

Botox and dermal fillers consistently generate the highest call volume. Laser hair removal, CoolSculpting, microneedling, and PRP treatments drive strong intent-based searches. We build separate campaigns for each treatment so someone searching for "lip filler cost" sees an ad specifically about lip enhancement, not a generic med spa ad. Higher-value treatments like body contouring and skin tightening have higher cost-per-click but also much higher revenue per patient.

How does the AI handle sensitive med spa inquiries and pricing questions?

The AI is trained to handle med spa calls with the appropriate professionalism and sensitivity. When callers ask about pricing, the AI acknowledges their interest, explains that pricing depends on the treatment plan, and encourages them to book a complimentary consultation for a personalized assessment. This approach matches how top med spas handle pricing inquiries — it doesn't give prices that scare people away but also doesn't dodge the question, instead creating a clear path to an in-person consultation where your team can close.

How do I get my med spa to rank higher on Google for aesthetic searches?

Local SEO for med spas requires a combination of Google Business Profile optimization, review generation, and treatment-specific content. We optimize your profile with accurate aesthetic service categories, before/after photos, and provider credentials. We build a steady flow of five-star reviews that mention specific treatments, create detailed treatment pages on your website, and ensure your med spa appears in aesthetic-specific directories. This positions you as the local authority for every treatment you offer.

Can this system help med spas build recurring revenue from existing patients?

Yes. While our primary focus is new patient acquisition, the AI Voice Agent plays a role in retention by handling rebooking calls efficiently and capturing treatment preferences so your team can proactively recommend follow-up appointments. Botox clients need re-treatment every three to four months, filler clients every six to twelve months. The system ensures that when they call to rebook, the appointment is captured immediately — not lost to voicemail.

How much should a med spa spend on Google Ads?

Med spa keywords are competitive, with cost-per-click ranging from $10 to $60 depending on the treatment and market. Botox and filler keywords are on the higher end, but the revenue per new patient justifies the investment — a single new Botox client generates $1,500 to $3,000 annually in recurring treatments. We recommend starting with a budget that generates 15 to 30 consultation bookings per month, then scaling based on your consultation-to-treatment conversion rate.

What makes Salois Digital different from other med spa marketing agencies?

Most med spa marketing agencies focus on Instagram aesthetics and social media content — which builds brand awareness but doesn't directly book consultations. Salois Digital builds the complete patient acquisition pipeline: Google Ads targeting high-intent treatment searches, local SEO for map pack visibility, and an AI Voice Agent that answers every call, handles pricing inquiries professionally, and books consultations 24/7. We track consultation bookings and patient revenue, not likes and engagement. The AI call handling alone sets us apart from every other med spa marketing agency.
