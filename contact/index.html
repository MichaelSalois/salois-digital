Questions About Getting Started

Here’s what small business owners usually want to know before reaching out.

Is the business audit really free?

Yes — completely free with no obligation. We’ll audit your website, Google Business Profile, SEO health, ad performance (if applicable), and call handling process. You’ll receive a branded PDF report with specific findings and actionable recommendations. Whether or not you choose to work with us, the audit is yours to keep and use however you’d like.

How quickly will I hear back after submitting the form?

Michael personally reviews every inquiry. You’ll hear back within one business day — and in most cases, within a few hours. No automated responses, no getting passed around to different people. You’ll talk directly with the person who will actually be managing your account.

What should I prepare before the consultation?

Nothing. Just tell us your business name, what industry you’re in, and what’s not working. We’ll handle the research from there. If you have access to your Google Ads dashboard or website analytics, that helps us give more specific recommendations — but it’s not required at all.

Will you try to hard-sell me on the call?

No. We’ll show you what we found in the audit, explain what we’d recommend, and give you transparent pricing. If it makes sense for your business, great. If not, no pressure — you still walk away with a valuable audit. We don’t do high-pressure sales because we don’t need to. Our results do the selling.

Do I need to be in a specific location to work with Salois Digital?

No. We work with local businesses across the entire United States. Everything we do — from the AI Voice Agent setup to Google Ads management to SEO — is handled remotely. Your customers are local, but your marketing partner doesn’t need to be in the same zip code to deliver results.

I’m not sure what I need. Can you help me figure it out?

That’s exactly what the free audit is for. Tell us what’s going on — maybe you’re not getting enough calls, or you’re getting calls but not converting them, or you don’t know if your current marketing is working. We’ll diagnose the problem and recommend the right solution, whether that’s one service or a full package.
