Frequently Asked Questions About Digital Marketing for Optometrists

Answers optometrists ask most about getting more patients and growing with AI.

How do optometry practices get more patients online?

The most effective approach combines Google Ads targeting patients searching for eye exams and eye care, local SEO for map pack visibility, a website with accepted insurance plans and online booking, and an AI Voice Agent that answers every call. Most optometry practices lose patients because phone lines are busy during peak hours. An AI that answers instantly converts those callers into booked exams.

What is the best way to market an optometry practice?

Google Ads targeting "eye exam near me," "optometrist accepting [insurance]," and "eye doctor [city]" reach patients ready to book. Combine with landing pages listing accepted vision plans, Google Business Profile with your eyewear selection and reviews, local SEO, and an AI Voice Agent that handles exam scheduling.

How much do Google Ads cost for optometrists?

Cost per click for optometry keywords typically ranges from $5 to $20 depending on market competition. With patient lifetime values including annual exams, glasses, and contacts worth $500 to $2,000+ annually, the ROI is strong. Salois Digital tracks cost per booked exam and new patient acquisition.

Why do optometry practices miss so many patient calls?

Optometry front desk staff handle frame selections, insurance verification, contact lens orders, and patient check-ins simultaneously. Phone lines get busy during peak hours. When patients are calling to book their annual exam, they won't wait on hold — they'll call the next optometrist. The AI Voice Agent eliminates hold times entirely.

How does the AI Voice Agent work for optometry practices?

When a patient calls and your staff is busy, the AI answers within two seconds. It asks about their vision plan, last exam date, current vision concerns, and scheduling preferences. It books a comprehensive eye exam directly into your scheduling system. It can also answer questions about accepted insurance plans, office hours, and services offered.

Is SEO worth it for optometry practices?

Yes. Patients search for optometrists locally, often triggered by insurance renewal or vision changes. Ranking for "eye doctor near me" and "optometrist accepting [vision plan]" generates consistent, qualified leads. We optimize your website with insurance-specific content and build your Google Business Profile to dominate local results.

How fast can an optometry practice start getting more patients?

Google Ads generate exam booking calls within the first week. Most practices see new patient exams within two weeks of launch. SEO builds over 60 to 90 days. The combination means you're filling exam slots from day one.

What makes Salois Digital different for optometry practices?

We understand the seasonal patterns of optometry — back-to-school rushes, insurance renewal periods, and New Year resolution bookings. Our system captures every lead during these surges: ads drive patients, the AI answers every call, and SEO builds year-round visibility. We track booked exams and new patients, not impressions.
