Frequently Asked Questions About Digital Marketing for Wedding Planners

Answers wedding planners business owners ask most about getting more bookings and growing with AI.

How do wedding planners get more clients online?

The most effective approach combines Google Ads targeting newly engaged couples searching for planners, local SEO so you appear in the map pack and organic results, a portfolio-driven website that showcases your best work, and an AI Voice Agent that answers every inquiry 24/7. Most wedding planners lose clients not because couples can't find them, but because inquiries come in during events or on weekends when they're unavailable. Fixing that response gap is the single biggest lever for booking more weddings.

What is the best way to advertise a wedding planning business?

Google Ads targeting high-intent searches like "wedding planner near me," "wedding coordinator in [city]," and "full-service wedding planner" put you in front of couples who are actively looking to hire right now. Combine that with Instagram and Facebook ads showcasing your portfolio, local SEO for map pack visibility, and an AI Voice Agent that turns every inquiry into a consultation — and you've built a system that captures leads from every channel.

How much do Google Ads cost for wedding planners?

Cost per click for wedding planning keywords typically ranges from $5 to $20 depending on your market and competition. Since the average wedding planning package can range from $3,000 to $15,000 or more, even a handful of booked clients per month represents an exceptional return on ad spend. The key is tracking cost per booked wedding, not just cost per click — which is exactly what Salois Digital measures.

Why do wedding planners miss so many client inquiries?

Wedding planners are literally at weddings during peak inquiry hours. Weekends and evenings — when engaged couples are most likely to browse and call — are exactly when planners are managing events. The AI Voice Agent solves this perfectly: it answers every call within two seconds, captures all the details you need, and books the consultation for a time when you're available.

How does the AI Voice Agent work for wedding planners?

When a couple calls and you're unavailable, the AI answers within two seconds. It greets them warmly, asks about their wedding date, venue preferences, estimated guest count, style vision, and budget range. It answers common questions about your packages and process, then books a consultation directly into your calendar. Every call is recorded and transcribed so you can review it before calling back. It works evenings, weekends, and holidays — exactly when engaged couples are planning.

Is SEO worth it for wedding planners?

Absolutely. Couples spend months researching and comparing planners online. When someone searches "best wedding planner in [your city]," SEO determines whether you show up on page one or get buried behind competitors. Local SEO is especially powerful because wedding planning is inherently location-based. We optimize your website, Google Business Profile, and review strategy to build visibility that generates free, qualified leads month after month.

How fast can a wedding planner start getting leads from digital marketing?

Google Ads can generate inquiries within the first week of launch. Most wedding planners see their first booked consultations within two weeks. SEO builds more gradually over 60 to 90 days, but the combination of paid ads and AI call handling means you're booking consultations from day one while organic visibility grows in the background.

What makes Salois Digital different from other marketing agencies for wedding planners?

Most agencies will post to your Instagram and call it marketing. Salois Digital builds a connected system: Google Ads drive engaged couples to your site, your portfolio-optimized landing pages convert them into inquiries, the AI Voice Agent answers every call and books consultations, and SEO compounds your visibility year over year. We track booked weddings, not likes or followers.
